# Product Guide - São Luís Garagem

## Initial Concept
Web application for Garagem São Luís, a certified auto repair shop in Oliveira de Azeméis, Portugal.

## Target Audience
- **Potential Customers:** Individuals in the Oliveira de Azeméis region and surrounding areas who are seeking a certified, professional, and trustworthy mechanic for their vehicle repairs and maintenance.

## Primary Goals
- **Online Visibility:** Establish and increase the digital presence of Garagem São Luís to reach a wider local audience and attract new clients through a professional online showcase.

## Key Features
- **Service Catalog:** A detailed and visually appealing section listing all automotive services offered, such as general mechanics, advanced diagnostics, and air conditioning maintenance.
- **Interactive Contact Form:** A functional and easy-to-use form that allows potential clients to request quotes or information, with automated email notifications sent directly to the workshop.
- **Location & Navigation:** Seamless integration with Google Maps alongside clear contact details to ensure customers can easily find and navigate to the physical shop.
